Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of purchase. Products must be unused and in original packaging.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs vary depending on destination.

How can I track my order?

After your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, and other major payment methods.

Can I change or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it by contacting our support team.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! Gift cards are available for purchase on our website. They can be used for any products.

How do I care for my hair extensions?

Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid excessive heat and brushing when wet to extend lifespan.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we provide special pricing for wholesale and bulk orders. Please contact our sales team for details.
